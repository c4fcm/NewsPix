Thank you for installing NewsPix for the Keene Sentinel

Each time you open a new tab, NewsPix will display a headline from the Keene Sentinel. You can try it out now by opening a new tab!

NewsPix is a suite of apps that drive engagement in local news through pictures and visualizations. It is conceived and developed by Matt Carroll (@mattatmit), Catherine D'Ignazio (@kanarinka) and Jay Vachon (@appleslerp). Content providers interested in using NewsPix for their own site should contact Matt Carroll.
