About NewsPix

NewsPix is a suite of apps that drive engagement in local news through pictures and visualizations. Our first app is a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox that delivers breaking news to the new tab window of a desktop user's browser. In Fall 2016, we will roll out a mobile version of NewsPix.

Our first pilot partner is the Sentinel Source out of Keene, NH, who has used NewsPix to engage their readers since 2014. In summer 2016, Asahi Shimbun, Japan's leading newspaper, rolled out a version of NewsPix to engage their readers with the national high school baseball tournament. Check it out in the Chrome store.
