About NewsPix

NewsPix is a suite of apps that drive engagement in local news through pictures and visualizations. Our first app is a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox that delivers breaking news to the new tab window of a desktop user's browser. Our first pilot partner is the Boston Herald.
